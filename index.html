關於 養車管家AI / About

養車管家AI 是一款 iOS app，透過 AI 自動辨識你的汽車維修與保養帳單，並依歷史紀錄提供費用合理性與保養週期建議。支援台灣與美國兩地的帳單格式，繁體中文 / English 介面可切換。

養車管家AI is an iOS app that uses AI to automatically scan car service receipts and tracks maintenance history. It supports both Taiwan and US receipt formats and can be used in Traditional Chinese or English.
